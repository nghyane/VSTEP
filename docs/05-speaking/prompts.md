### Scenario 1: Gift for Best Student

**Tình huống:** Your cousin is the best student of the class this year. His parents are thinking about a present. There are three options:
- A laptop
- A holiday
- Some money

**Which option would you suggest?**

**Mẫu trả lời:**
> "If I were in this situation, I would suggest a holiday as the best choice among the three options. Firstly, a holiday would give my cousin a well-deserved break after a year of hard work. Traveling can broaden the mind and help reduce stress. Secondly, a holiday creates lasting memories that are more valuable than material things.
>
> While a laptop might seem practical, my cousin may already have one or could become distracted by games and social media. As for money, teenagers often don't know how to manage it wisely.
>
> Therefore, I believe a holiday is the most rewarding gift for such an achievement."

## Part 3: Topic Development - Đề Mẫu

### Topic 1: Working in Pairs

**Thẻ chủ đề:**
> Working in pairs helps students learn better at school.
> - Share ideas
> - Become more active
> - Improve communication skills
> - (Your own ideas)

**Mẫu trả lời B1:**
> "I would like to talk about working in pairs at school.
>
> In my opinion, working in pairs helps students learn better. First, it gives students the chance to learn from each other. When students work together, they can share ideas and explain the lesson in a simple way.
>
> Second, pair work helps students become more active in class. They can discuss, ask questions, and take part in the lesson instead of only listening to the teacher.
>
> In addition, working in pairs improves students' communication and social skills. They learn how to cooperate and respect different opinions.
>
> In short, I think working in pairs is a useful method and should be used more often at school."

**Mẫu trả lời B2:**
> "I would like to discuss whether working in pairs helps students learn better at school.
>
> From my point of view, pair work has several significant benefits. Firstly, it allows students to learn from each other through sharing ideas and knowledge. This peer-to-peer learning often makes concepts clearer because students explain things in ways their classmates can understand.
>
> Secondly, working in pairs encourages students to be more active participants in their education. Rather than passively listening to lectures, they engage in discussions, debate ideas, and take ownership of their learning.
>
> Moreover, pair work develops essential social skills such as communication, collaboration, and the ability to consider different perspectives. These skills are invaluable for future careers.
>
> However, I would acknowledge that pair work should be implemented thoughtfully, as some students may dominate while others remain passive.
>
> Overall, I firmly believe that when properly organized, pair work is an effective teaching method that enhances both academic learning and personal development."

## Cấu Trúc Trả Lời Đề Xuất

### Part 2 Template

```
INTRODUCTION (10-15 seconds):
"In this situation, I would choose [OPTION] for several important reasons."

BODY (1.5-2 minutes):
"Firstly, [reason 1 + explanation + example].

Secondly, [reason 2 + explanation + example].

Now, let me explain why I didn't choose the other options.
[Option 2] might seem attractive, but [reason against it].
Similarly, [Option 3] has the drawback of [reason against it]."

CONCLUSION (10-15 seconds):
"Therefore, I firmly believe that [OPTION] is the best choice in this situation."
```

### Part 3 Template

```
INTRODUCTION (15-20 seconds):
"I would like to talk about [TOPIC].
In my opinion, [STATE YOUR MAIN VIEW]."

BODY (2-2.5 minutes):
"Firstly, [POINT 1 from card + explanation + example].
This is important because [reason].

Secondly, [POINT 2 from card + explanation + example].
For instance, [specific example].

Additionally, [POINT 3 from card OR your own idea + explanation].

Furthermore, I would like to add that [YOUR OWN IDEA]."

CONCLUSION (15-20 seconds):
"In conclusion, I strongly believe that [RESTATE MAIN VIEW].
[Final recommendation or thought for the future]."
```

## Mẹo Quan Trọng

1. **Part 1**: Mở rộng câu trả lời với chi tiết và ví dụ
2. **Part 2**: Dùng đủ 1 phút chuẩn bị, lập dàn ý nhanh
3. **Part 3**: Bao quát TẤT CẢ các điểm trong thẻ + thêm ý kiến riêng
4. **Mọi Part**: Sử dụng linking words, giữ dòng chảy tự nhiên
