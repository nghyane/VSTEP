# VSTEP Writing Task 2 (Essay) - Phân tích chi tiết

## Tổng quan

| Thông tin | Chi tiết |
|-----------|----------|
| **Thời gian** | 40 phút |
| **Số từ tối thiểu** | 250 từ |
| **Trọng số điểm** | 2/3 tổng điểm Writing |
| **Levels kiểm tra** | B1, B2, C1 (CEFR) |

---

## Các dạng Essay

### 1. Opinion/Agree-Disagree Essays

**Cách nhận biết:**
- "Do you agree or disagree?"
- "To what extent do you agree?"
- "What is your opinion?"
- "What do you think?"

#### Sample Prompts

1. **Environmental Responsibility**
   > "Rainforests play an important role in improving the environment, especially the air quality. Many people think that individual effort to improve the air quality is not enough and it is the responsibility of the government to reduce air pollution. Do you agree or disagree with this opinion?"

2. **English Teaching**
   > "Some people think that children should study English with native teachers. Others believe that they should study English with Vietnamese English teachers. Do you agree or disagree?"

3. **Online vs Traditional Education** *(Đề thi 2024)*
   > "Online education is becoming increasingly popular. Some people believe it is an effective replacement for traditional classroom learning, while others think it cannot provide the same quality of education. Write an essay giving your opinion."

4. **Dangerous Sports**
   > "Some people think that governments should ban dangerous sports, while others believe people should have freedom to do any sport they choose. What is your opinion?"

5. **Volunteer Work** *(ĐH Văn Lang 2024)*
   > "Volunteer work is an important activity that brings many benefits to both individuals and communities. To what extent do you agree with this statement?"

6. **Smoking Ban** *(ĐH Sư Phạm TPHCM 12/2024)*
   > "Should smoking be banned in public places? Give reasons and examples to support your opinion."

#### Cấu trúc

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Background + Thesis (quan điểm rõ ràng) | ~40-50 từ |
| **Body 1** | Lý do 1 + Giải thích + Ví dụ | ~80-100 từ |
| **Body 2** | Lý do 2 + Giải thích + Ví dụ | ~80-100 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Nhắc lại quan điểm + Tóm tắt | ~30-40 từ |

#### Template

**Introduction:**
> *In recent years, [topic] has become a matter of considerable debate. While some people believe that [opposing view], I firmly believe that [your position]. This essay will outline the reasons for my opinion.*

**Body Paragraph:**
> *First(ly)/Second(ly), [main reason]. This is because [explanation]. For example, [specific example]. Therefore, [connect back to thesis].*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, although [acknowledge opposing view briefly], I strongly believe that [restate your position] because [summarize key reasons].*

### 2. Discussion Essays (Discuss Both Views)

**Cách nhận biết:**
- "Discuss both views and give your opinion"
- "Some people think... while others argue..."
- "Discuss both sides of this argument"

#### Sample Prompts

1. **Celebrity Privacy**
   > "Some people think that celebrities should be given more privacy. Others believe that the price of their fame is an invasion into their private lives. Discuss both views and give your opinion."

2. **Computers in Education**
   > "Computers are being used more and more in education. Some people say that this is a positive trend, while others argue that it is leading to negative consequences. Discuss both sides and give your opinion."

3. **Tourism Impact** *(Đề thi 2024)*
   > "Some people believe that tourism is beneficial because it brings income and jobs to local communities. Others argue that it causes environmental damage and cultural loss. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."

4. **Technology at Work** *(Đề thi 2025)*
   > "Some people think that the increasing use of technology at work improves productivity and efficiency. Others are concerned that it replaces human jobs and increases unemployment. Discuss both sides."

5. **AI Impact** *(ĐH Văn Lang 12/2024)*
   > "Discuss the impact of artificial intelligence (AI) on daily life and work. Does AI bring more benefits or cause more harm?"

6. **Social Media and Memories** *(ĐH Ngân Hàng TPHCM 12/2024)*
   > "Many people post their memories on social media. Discuss the benefits and drawbacks of this habit."

#### Cấu trúc

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Paraphrase topic + Nêu cả 2 views + Quan điểm của bạn | ~40-50 từ |
| **Body 1** | View bạn ủng hộ (Lý do + Giải thích + Ví dụ) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Body 2** | Opposing view (Lý do + Giải thích + Phản biện) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Tóm tắt + View nào mạnh hơn | ~30-40 từ |

#### Template

**Introduction:**
> *[Topic] is a controversial issue that has sparked debate. While there are certain people who claim that [View A], others espouse the idea that [View B]. Both sides have their merits; however, I believe the [former/latter] view is more reasonable.*

**Body 1 (View bạn ủng hộ):**
> *On the one hand, proponents of [view A] argue that [reason]. This can be explained by [explanation]. For instance, [example]. Thus, [connect to main point].*

**Body 2 (Opposing view):**
> *On the other hand, those who support [view B] claim that [reason]. However, this argument can be refuted because [rebuttal].*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, although there are valid reasons why [opposing view], it is still more reasonable to conclude that [your supported view].*

### 3. Problem-Solution Essays

**Cách nhận biết:**
- "What are the problems and solutions?"
- "Discuss the causes and suggest measures"
- "What solutions can you suggest?"
- "How can this problem be solved?"

#### Sample Prompts

1. **Age Discrimination**
   > "In some countries, it can be very difficult for people over the age of 50 to get good jobs, despite their experience. What do you think are the causes of this problem and what measures could be taken to solve it?"

2. **Traffic Congestion** *(Đề thi 2024)*
   > "Traffic congestion has become a serious issue in many large cities around the world. Discuss the problems caused by traffic congestion and suggest possible solutions."

3. **Sky Train Solution** *(Học viện Báo chí 01/2024)*
   > "One way to solve the problem of congestion in cities is to build sky trains which run overhead rather than on or under the ground. What are the advantages and disadvantages of this approach?"

4. **Brain Drain** *(ĐH KHXH&NV 03/2024)*
   > "A growing number of professionals, including doctors and teachers, are leaving their own poorer countries to work in developed countries. What are the reasons behind this trend? What solutions can be proposed?"

5. **Youth Unemployment**
   > "Many young people today struggle to find employment despite having university degrees. What are the causes of this problem and what solutions can address this issue?"

#### Cấu trúc

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Background + Nêu essay thảo luận problems và solutions | ~40-50 từ |
| **Body 1** | Problems/Causes (1-2 vấn đề chính với giải thích) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Body 2** | Solutions (1-2 giải pháp thực tế với giải thích) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Tóm tắt + Khuyến nghị cuối | ~30-40 từ |

#### Template

**Introduction:**
> *[Topic] is a pressing issue facing society today. This problem has serious implications for [affected groups/areas]. The following essay will analyze the principal factors leading to this problem and then offer some potential ways to address it.*

**Body 1 (Problems):**
> *One of the main causes of this issue is [cause]. This can be attributed to [explanation]. As a result, [effect]. Furthermore, [second cause if applicable].*

**Body 2 (Solutions):**
> *To tackle this problem, several measures can be implemented. Firstly, [solution 1 + how it works]. Additionally, [solution 2 + explanation]. These steps would help to [positive outcome].*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, while [problem] poses significant challenges, it can be successfully addressed through [summary of solutions]. Governments and individuals must work together to implement these measures.*

### 4. Cause-Effect Essays

**Cách nhận biết:**
- "What are the causes and effects?"
- "Discuss the reasons and impacts"
- "What has led to this situation and what are the consequences?"

#### Sample Prompts

1. **Overpopulation**
   > "Overpopulation is a global problem of today. It has reached an alarming rate and demands a quick remedy. Discuss the causes and effects of this issue."

2. **Job-Hopping Trend**
   > "Recent years have witnessed a widespread increase in the change of jobs and careers among youngsters. Discuss the reasons for this trend. Is this a positive or negative development?"

3. **Cheating in Exams**
   > "Cheating in school exams has been a serious concern since time immemorial. Why do you think children cheat in exams? What are the long-term effects if this ubiquitous practice is tolerated?"

4. **Youth English-Mixing** *(ĐH Kinh Tế Tài Chính 12/2024)*
   > "Many young people today insert English words when speaking Vietnamese. Discuss the reasons and impacts of this trend in modern society."

5. **Deforestation**
   > "Deforestation is occurring at an alarming rate across the globe. What are the main causes of this problem and what effects does it have on the environment and society?"

#### Cấu trúc

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Background + Nêu essay phân tích causes và effects | ~40-50 từ |
| **Body 1** | Causes/Reasons (2-3 nguyên nhân chính với giải thích) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Body 2** | Effects/Consequences (2-3 hệ quả với giải thích) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Tóm tắt + Đánh giá tổng thể | ~30-40 từ |

#### Template

**Introduction:**
> *[Topic] has become an increasingly concerning phenomenon in recent years. After elaborating on the causes of this important issue, its effects on society will be examined.*

**Body 1 (Causes):**
> *This situation can be attributed to several factors. First, [cause 1 + explanation]. Additionally, [cause 2 + explanation]. This has led to [transition to effects].*

**Body 2 (Effects):**
> *As a consequence of these factors, society is now facing various challenges. One vital consequence is [effect 1]. Moreover, [effect 2]. These effects have significant implications for [broader impact].*

### 5. Advantages-Disadvantages Essays

**Cách nhận biết:**
- "What are the advantages and disadvantages?"
- "Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?"
- "Discuss the benefits and drawbacks"

#### Sample Prompts

1. **Multiple Textbooks**
   > "Many developed countries use more than one set of textbooks in their schools. What are the benefits of having multiple sets of textbooks in education?"

2. **Working from Home**
   > "The Internet is widespread nowadays, letting people work at home instead of in their office. What are the benefits and drawbacks of working at home to employees and employers?"

3. **Part-time Jobs for Students** *(Đề thi 2024)*
   > "Many university students take part-time jobs while studying. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of having a part-time job while being a student."

4. **Being Famous** *(ĐH Ngoại ngữ ĐHĐN 08/2024)*
   > "What are the pros and cons of being famous?"

5. **Gap Year**
   > "Some students take a year off between high school and university. What are the advantages and disadvantages of taking a gap year?"

6. **Alternative Energy** *(ĐH Hà Nội 12/2024)*
   > "Discuss the role of alternative energy sources. What are their advantages and disadvantages?"

#### Cấu trúc

| Phần | Nội dung | Số từ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Introduction** | Background + Nêu essay thảo luận pros và cons | ~40-50 từ |
| **Body 1** | Advantages (2-3 lợi ích với ví dụ) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Body 2** | Disadvantages (2-3 hạn chế với ví dụ) | ~80-100 từ |
| **Conclusion** | Tóm tắt + Đánh giá tổng thể của bạn | ~30-40 từ |

#### Template

**Introduction:**
> *In recent years, [topic] has become increasingly common/popular. While this trend offers several benefits, it also presents certain drawbacks. This essay will examine both the advantages and disadvantages of [topic].*

**Body 1 (Advantages):**
> *There are numerous benefits to [topic]. Firstly, [advantage 1 + explanation + example]. Furthermore, [advantage 2 + explanation]. These factors demonstrate that [positive statement].*

**Body 2 (Disadvantages):**
> *However, [topic] also has notable drawbacks. One significant disadvantage is [disadvantage 1 + explanation]. Additionally, [disadvantage 2 + example]. These factors must be carefully considered.*

**Conclusion:**
> *In conclusion, while [topic] offers [key advantages], the [disadvantages] cannot be ignored. On balance, I believe that [your evaluation - e.g., "the benefits outweigh the drawbacks if proper measures are taken"].*

## Từ nối theo chức năng

| Chức năng | Từ/Cụm từ |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Thêm thông tin** | In addition, Furthermore, Moreover, What is more, Additionally, Also, Besides |
| **Đối lập** | However, Nevertheless, On the other hand, In contrast, Although, Despite, While, Whereas, Conversely |
| **Ví dụ** | For example, For instance, Such as, To illustrate, In particular, Specifically |
| **Nguyên nhân** | Because, Since, Due to, Owing to, As a result of, This is caused by |
| **Kết quả** | Therefore, Thus, Consequently, As a result, Hence, This leads to, For this reason |
| **Thứ tự** | Firstly, Secondly, Finally, To begin with, Next, Subsequently, Ultimately |
| **Quan điểm** | In my opinion, I believe that, I think, From my perspective, It seems to me |
| **Kết luận** | In conclusion, To sum up, In summary, To conclude, On balance, Overall |

### Cụm từ chuyên biệt theo dạng essay

**Cho Cause-Effect:**
- This problem is caused by...
- This situation can be attributed to...
- One rationale that can be given to explain this is...
- One vital consequence of this is...

**Cho Problem-Solution:**
- The problem can be successfully prevented by...
- There are several concrete steps we can take to combat this threat...
- These measures could be implemented to address...

**Cho Discussion Essays:**
- There are certain people who claim that...
- In contrast, other individuals espouse the idea that...
- Both sides have their own merits; however...

## Yêu cầu theo Level

### B1 Level Essay

**Task Fulfillment:**
- Đáp ứng một phần yêu cầu
- Quan điểm có thể chưa rõ ràng xuyên suốt
- Ý chính có nhưng chưa phát triển đầy đủ

**Organization:**
- Cấu trúc essay cơ bản
- Chia đoạn có thể chưa nhất quán
- Dùng từ nối đơn giản (and, but, because, so)

**Vocabulary:**
- Từ vựng cơ bản, có thể kiểm soát
- Phạm vi từ hạn chế; lặp lại nhiều
- ~200-230 từ

**Grammar:**
- Cấu trúc đơn giản chính xác
- Câu phức cố gắng nhưng có lỗi
- Thì cơ bản dùng đúng

**Đặc điểm B1 Essay:**
- ✓ 4 đoạn (intro, 2 body, conclusion)
- ✓ Câu đơn chiếm đa số
- ✓ Từ nối cơ bản (First, Second, In conclusion)
- ✓ Từ vựng thông thường về chủ đề quen thuộc
- ✓ ~200-230 từ

---

### B2 Level Essay

**Task Fulfillment:**
- Đề cập tất cả các phần của đề
- Quan điểm rõ ràng xuyên suốt
- Ý chính liên quan và phát triển với chi tiết hỗ trợ

**Organization:**
- Cấu trúc essay rõ ràng
- Mỗi đoạn có topic central rõ
- Sử dụng đa dạng cohesive devices hiệu quả

**Vocabulary:**
- Phạm vi từ vựng đủ rộng
- Sử dụng từ ít phổ biến phù hợp
- Biết về collocation
- ~250-280 từ

**Grammar:**
- Sử dụng đa dạng cấu trúc phức
- Tạo ra câu không lỗi thường xuyên
- Kiểm soát ngữ pháp tốt, lỗi không nhiều

**Đặc điểm B2 Essay:**
- ✓ Essay 4 đoạn có cấu trúc tốt
- ✓ Topic sentences trong mỗi body paragraph
- ✓ Đa dạng từ nối (Moreover, Nevertheless, Therefore)
- ✓ Một số từ vựng học thuật
- ✓ Câu phức với mệnh đề quan hệ, điều kiện
- ✓ ~250-280 từ

---

### C1 Level Essay

**Task Fulfillment:**
- Đề cập đầy đủ tất cả khía cạnh của đề
- Quan điểm phát triển hoàn toàn
- Ý tưởng phát triển tốt với ví dụ liên quan
- Hỗ trợ các điểm chính thuyết phục

**Organization:**
- Tổ chức khéo léo với progression rõ ràng
- Sử dụng đa dạng cohesive devices
- Chia đoạn được quản lý tốt

**Vocabulary:**
- Phạm vi từ vựng rộng với linh hoạt và chính xác
- Sử dụng lexical items không phổ biến với style
- Lỗi hiếm trong word choice
- Thể hiện word choice tinh vi

**Grammar:**
- Đa dạng cấu trúc sử dụng chính xác
- Lỗi hiếm và khó phát hiện
- Kiểm soát tốt ngôn ngữ phức tạp
- Linh hoạt hoàn toàn trong cấu trúc câu

**Đặc điểm C1 Essay:**
- ✓ Cấu trúc 4-5 đoạn tinh vi
- ✓ Lập luận có sắc thái với counterpoints
- ✓ Cohesive devices nâng cao (Furthermore, Nevertheless, Notwithstanding)
- ✓ Từ vựng học thuật/formal
- ✓ Cấu trúc ngữ pháp phức tạp (inversion, cleft sentences, participle clauses)
- ✓ ~280-320 từ

## Checklist

### Trước khi viết (5-10 phút):
- [ ] Xác định dạng essay từ từ khóa trong đề
- [ ] Brainstorm 2-3 ý chính
- [ ] Tạo outline ngắn gọn
- [ ] Ghi chú từ vựng quan trọng cho chủ đề

### Trong khi viết (25-30 phút):
- [ ] Introduction: Background + Thesis/Position (~40-50 từ)
- [ ] Body 1: Điểm chính 1 + Support + Example (~80-100 từ)
- [ ] Body 2: Điểm chính 2 + Support + Example (~80-100 từ)
- [ ] Conclusion: Nhắc lại quan điểm + Summary (~30-40 từ)

### Sau khi viết (5 phút):
- [ ] Kiểm tra số từ (tối thiểu 250 từ)
- [ ] Xác nhận essay trả lời đúng câu hỏi
- [ ] Kiểm tra ngữ pháp và chính tả
- [ ] Đảm bảo chia đoạn đúng
- [ ] Xác nhận có sử dụng từ nối
