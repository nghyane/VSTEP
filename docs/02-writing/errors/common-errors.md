# VSTEP Writing - Common Errors (Lỗi Thường Gặp)

> **Đối tượng**: Thí sinh VSTEP người Việt
> **Mục đích**: Nhận diện và khắc phục lỗi phổ biến trong Writing

---

## 1. Grammar Errors (Lỗi Ngữ Pháp)

### 1.1 Subject-Verb Agreement (Hòa hợp Chủ-Vị)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---------|------|------------|
| *Everyone have their own opinion.* | Everyone **has** their own opinion. | "Everyone" là số ít |
| *The number of students are increasing.* | The number of students **is** increasing. | "The number of" + động từ số ít |
| *A lot of problems has occurred.* | A lot of problems **have** occurred. | "problems" là số nhiều |
| *News are important.* | News **is** important. | "News" là danh từ không đếm được |
| *The government have decided.* | The government **has** decided. | "Government" là tập thể, số ít |

**Rule cần nhớ:**
```
Số ít: Everyone, anyone, someone, nobody, each, every + N
Số ít: The number of, a lot of + uncountable
Số nhiều: A number of, a lot of + countable plural
```

### 1.2 Article Errors (Lỗi Mạo Từ)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---------|------|------------|
| *I bought laptop yesterday.* | I bought **a** laptop yesterday. | Danh từ đếm được số ít cần a/an |
| *The money is important.* | **Money** is important. | Nói chung, không dùng "the" |
| *I love the nature.* | I love **nature**. | "Nature" nói chung, không cần the |
| *She is doctor.* | She is **a** doctor. | Nghề nghiệp cần a/an |
| *A honesty is important.* | **Honesty** is important. | Danh từ trừu tượng không cần a |

**Nguyên tắc:**
```
THE: khi người đọc biết đang nói về cái gì cụ thể
A/AN: khi giới thiệu lần đầu, một trong nhiều
ZERO ARTICLE: nói chung, danh từ trừu tượng, không đếm được
```

### 1.3 Tense Errors (Lỗi Thì)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---------|------|------------|
| *Yesterday, I go to school.* | Yesterday, I **went** to school. | Quá khứ đơn với "yesterday" |
| *I am living here for 5 years.* | I **have lived** here for 5 years. | Present perfect với "for" |
| *By next year, I will finish my degree.* | By next year, I **will have finished** my degree. | Future perfect |
| *When I was young, I am very active.* | When I was young, I **was** very active. | Nhất quán past tense |

**Timeline:**
```
Past Perfect ← Past Simple ← Present Perfect ← Present ← Future
     had V3           V2          have V3         V1      will V
```

### 1.4 Preposition Errors (Lỗi Giới Từ)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Collocation |
|---------|------|-------------|
| *depend of* | depend **on** | depend on something |
| *different with* | different **from** | different from X |
| *good in* | good **at** | good at doing something |
| *interested about* | interested **in** | interested in something |
| *discuss about* | discuss *(no prep)* | discuss something (không cần about) |
| *emphasize on* | emphasize *(no prep)* | emphasize something |
| *lack of* | lack *(no prep)* hoặc **a** lack of | We lack resources / There is a lack of resources |

**Verb + Preposition phổ biến:**
```
depend ON, rely ON, focus ON, concentrate ON
result IN, succeed IN, participate IN
contribute TO, lead TO, refer TO
consist OF, approve OF, think OF
prevent FROM, suffer FROM, benefit FROM
```

### 1.5 Sentence Fragments & Run-ons

#### Fragments (Câu thiếu thành phần)
| Lỗi Sai | Đúng |
|---------|------|
| *Because I was tired.* | I didn't go out **because I was tired**. |
| *Which is very important.* | Education, **which is very important**, should be free. |
| *Running every day.* | **Running every day** is good for health. |

#### Run-on Sentences (Câu chạy)
| Lỗi Sai | Đúng |
|---------|------|
| *I like coffee, I drink it every day.* | I like coffee**, so** I drink it every day. |
| *The weather was bad we stayed home.* | The weather was bad**,** **so** we stayed home. |

**Cách sửa Run-on:**
1. Thêm conjunction: and, but, so, because
2. Dùng semicolon: Coffee is good; I drink it daily.
3. Tách thành 2 câu

## 2. Vocabulary Errors (Lỗi Từ Vựng)

### 2.1 Word Choice (Chọn từ sai)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|---------|------|------------|
| *make a research* | **conduct/do** research | "make" không đi với "research" |
| *do a mistake* | **make** a mistake | "do" không đi với "mistake" |
| *very unique* | **unique** (no very) | "unique" đã là tuyệt đối |
| *more better* | **better** | Không dùng "more" với "better" |
| *very exhausted* | **exhausted** | "exhausted" đã có nghĩa "very tired" |

### 2.2 Word Form (Sai dạng từ)

| Lỗi Sai | Đúng | Word Form |
|---------|------|-----------|
| *This is importance.* | This is **important**. | Cần tính từ, không phải danh từ |
| *It is difficulty to...* | It is **difficult** to... | Cần tính từ sau "is" |
| *The develop of technology* | The **development** of technology | Sau "the" + "of" cần danh từ |
| *Think creative* | Think **creatively** | Sau động từ cần trạng từ |
| *Economic develop* | Economic **development** | Sau tính từ cần danh từ |

**Chú ý dạng từ:**
```
Noun: -tion, -ment, -ness, -ity, -ance/-ence
Adjective: -ful, -less, -ive, -al, -ous
Adverb: -ly
Verb: -ize, -ify, -en
```

### 2.3 False Friends (Từ dễ nhầm)

| Vietnamese Thinking | English Reality |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| *actual* (thực tế) | actual = hiện tại (dùng "real" cho "thực tế") |
| *sensible* (nhạy cảm) | sensible = hợp lý (dùng "sensitive" cho nhạy cảm) |
| *sympathetic* (cảm thông) | sympathetic = thông cảm (đúng!) |
| *eventually* (cuối cùng) | eventually = rốt cuộc, chắc chắn sẽ |
| *absolutely* (tuyệt đối) | absolutely = hoàn toàn (đúng nhưng overused) |

### 2.4 Overused Words

| Overused | Better Alternatives |
|----------|---------------------|
| good | beneficial, advantageous, favorable, positive |
| bad | detrimental, harmful, adverse, negative |
| big | significant, substantial, considerable |
| important | crucial, vital, essential, paramount |
| many | numerous, various, a multitude of |
| get | obtain, acquire, receive, achieve |
| make | create, generate, produce, establish |
| thing | aspect, factor, element, phenomenon |

## 3. Organization Errors (Lỗi Tổ Chức)

### 3.1 Missing Thesis Statement

**Sai:**
```
Education is important for people. There are many schools in Vietnam. 
Teachers work very hard.
```

**Đúng:**
```
Education plays a crucial role in personal and national development. 
This essay will argue that investing in education is the most 
effective way to improve a country's future.
```

### 3.2 Missing Topic Sentences

**Sai:**
```
[Body paragraph without clear focus]
Many students go to university. Some people think it is expensive. 
Knowledge is important. Jobs require degrees.
```

**Đúng:**
```
[Clear topic sentence]
Higher education provides graduates with improved career opportunities. 
Companies increasingly require degrees for professional positions. 
For example, data from the Ministry of Education shows that...
```

### 3.3 Weak Conclusion

**Sai:**
```
In conclusion, I talked about education. It has both advantages 
and disadvantages. Thank you for reading.
```

**Đúng:**
```
In conclusion, while education requires significant investment, 
its benefits for both individuals and society far outweigh the costs. 
Governments should prioritize funding for accessible, quality education 
to ensure long-term economic prosperity.
```

### 3.4 Poor Cohesion (Thiếu liên kết)

**Sai:**
```
Many people use smartphones. Screen time affects health. 
Children should play outside. Parents worry about their kids.
```

**Đúng:**
```
Many people now use smartphones extensively in their daily lives. 
As a result, excessive screen time has become a significant health 
concern. Consequently, health experts recommend that children should 
spend more time playing outside. This issue has understandably led 
many parents to worry about their children's wellbeing.
```

## 4. Task Response Errors (Lỗi Đáp Ứng Yêu Cầu)

### 4.1 Off-Topic (Lạc đề)

**Đề bài:** *Should students wear school uniforms?*

**Sai:** (Viết về tầm quan trọng của giáo dục)
```
Education is very important for young people. Schools should 
hire good teachers. Students should study hard...
```

**Đúng:**
```
Schools should require students to wear uniforms because it creates 
equality among students and reduces distractions...
```

### 4.2 Missing Bullet Points (Task 1)

**Đề bài requires:**
- Describe the problem
- Explain effects
- Suggest solutions

**Sai:** (Chỉ mô tả, không đề xuất giải pháp)
```
There is a problem with noise in my neighborhood. The noise is 
very loud. It comes from construction. I am very upset.
```

**Đúng:**
```
I am writing about the noise problem... [describe]
This has caused me to lose sleep and feel stressed... [effects]
I would suggest you contact the construction company to limit 
working hours to daytime only... [solutions]
```

### 4.3 Wrong Register

**Task 1 - Formal letter**

**Sai (quá informal):**
```
Hey there,
Just wanna tell you that your hotel was really bad. 
Fix it ASAP!
Cheers
```

**Đúng:**
```
Dear Sir/Madam,
I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the accommodation 
at your hotel. I would appreciate it if you could address this 
matter promptly.
Yours faithfully,
```

## 5. Vietnamese-Specific Errors

### 5.1 Direct Translation from Vietnamese

| Vietnamese | Wrong English | Correct English |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Tôi rất thích ăn phở | *I very like eat pho* | I really like eating pho |
| Anh ấy cao hơn tôi | *He tall more than me* | He is taller than me |
| Tôi 20 tuổi | *I 20 years old* | I am 20 years old |
| Ngày mai tôi đi học | *Tomorrow I go school* | I will go to school tomorrow |
| Cái này là của tôi | *This is of me* | This is mine |

### 5.2 Missing Linking Verbs

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|------|------------|
| *She beautiful.* | She **is** beautiful. | Cần linking verb "is" |
| *They very happy.* | They **are** very happy. | Tiếng Việt không có "to be" |
| *The food delicious.* | The food **is** delicious. | Tính từ cần linking verb |

### 5.3 No Plural Form

| Sai | Đúng | Giải thích |
|-----|------|------------|
| *Many student go to school.* | Many student**s** go to school. | "Many" + plural |
| *Two book* | Two book**s** | Số đếm + plural |
| *Several problem* | Several problem**s** | "Several" + plural |

### 5.4 Confused Tenses from Vietnamese Context

Vietnamese không có chia thì, nên:

| Vietnamese | Sai | Đúng |
|------------|-----|------|
| Tôi ăn sáng xong rồi | *I eat breakfast* | I **have eaten** breakfast |
| Hôm qua tôi đi chợ | *Yesterday I go market* | Yesterday I **went** to the market |
| Năm sau tôi tốt nghiệp | *Next year I graduate* | Next year I **will graduate** |

## 6. Punctuation Errors

### 6.1 Comma Errors

| Lỗi | Đúng | Rule |
|-----|------|------|
| *However I think...* | However**,** I think... | Dấu phẩy sau transition words |
| *I like apples, and oranges* | I like apples and oranges | Không cần phẩy trước "and" trong list 2 items |
| *If you study hard you will pass* | If you study hard**,** you will pass | Phẩy sau mệnh đề điều kiện đầu câu |

### 6.2 Run-on with Comma

**Comma splice (SAI):**
```
I like coffee, I drink it every day.
```

**Đúng:**
```
I like coffee, so I drink it every day.
I like coffee; I drink it every day.
I like coffee. I drink it every day.
```

## Checklist Trước Khi Nộp Bài

### Grammar
- [ ] Subject-verb agreement
- [ ] Articles (a, an, the, zero)
- [ ] Tense consistency
- [ ] Prepositions
- [ ] Complete sentences (no fragments/run-ons)

### Vocabulary
- [ ] Correct collocations
- [ ] Correct word forms
- [ ] No repetition (use synonyms)
- [ ] Appropriate formality

### Organization
- [ ] Clear thesis statement
- [ ] Topic sentences in each body paragraph
- [ ] Logical flow with linking words
- [ ] Strong conclusion

### Task Response
- [ ] Answered ALL parts of the question
- [ ] Stayed on topic
- [ ] Met word count requirement
- [ ] Appropriate register (formal/semi-formal)

## Self-Editing Strategy

### 1. First Read: Content
- Có trả lời đúng câu hỏi không?
- Có đủ ý không?
- Có ví dụ không?

### 2. Second Read: Organization
- Có thesis statement không?
- Mỗi paragraph có topic sentence không?
- Có linking words không?

### 3. Third Read: Grammar
- Subject-verb agreement
- Tenses
- Articles

### 4. Fourth Read: Vocabulary
- Word choice
- Collocations
- Spelling

**Tip:** Đọc ngược từ câu cuối lên đầu → Dễ phát hiện lỗi hơn vì không bị cuốn theo nghĩa.
