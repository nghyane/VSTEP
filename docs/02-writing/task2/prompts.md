# VSTEP Writing Task 2 - Prompt Bank (Ngân Hàng Đề)

> **Task 2**: Essay | **Thời gian**: 40 phút | **Số từ**: 250+

---

## 1. Education (Giáo dục)

### Opinion Essays

#### Prompt 1.1
> Some people believe that students should be allowed to choose what subjects they want to study at school. Others think that all students should follow the same curriculum. **Do you agree or disagree?**

**Thesis options:**
- Agree with choice: personalized learning, motivation
- Disagree: balanced education, national standards

---

#### Prompt 1.2
> Many people believe that university education should be free for all students. **To what extent do you agree or disagree?**

**Key arguments:**
- For: equal opportunity, skilled workforce
- Against: government costs, value appreciation

---

#### Prompt 1.3
> Some people think that schools should focus on academic subjects and that practical subjects such as art and music are a waste of time. **Do you agree or disagree?**

**Key arguments:**
- For arts: creativity, well-rounded education
- Against arts: job market demands

---

### Discussion Essays

#### Prompt 1.4
> Some people think that children should start formal education at a very early age. Others believe that they should not start school until they are older (around 6-7). **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**

**Points for each view:**
- Early start: cognitive development, social skills
- Later start: childhood freedom, readiness

---

#### Prompt 1.5
> Some parents choose to teach their children at home rather than sending them to school. **What are the advantages and disadvantages of home schooling?**

**Points:**
- Advantages: personalized pace, safety
- Disadvantages: socialization, parent qualifications

---

### Problem-Solution Essays

#### Prompt 1.6
> In many countries, students are losing interest in studying science subjects. **What are the reasons for this? What measures can be taken to address this problem?**

**Causes:** difficulty, boring teaching, career perception
**Solutions:** better teachers, practical labs, career awareness

---

## 2. Technology (Công nghệ)

### Opinion Essays

#### Prompt 2.1
> Some people believe that technology has made our lives too complicated. **Do you agree or disagree?**

**Key arguments:**
- Agree: information overload, dependency, stress
- Disagree: convenience, connectivity, efficiency

---

#### Prompt 2.2
> Many jobs in the future will be done by robots and artificial intelligence. **Is this a positive or negative development?**

**Points:**
- Positive: efficiency, new jobs, freeing humans
- Negative: unemployment, inequality, loss of skills

---

### Discussion Essays

#### Prompt 2.3
> Some people think that social media has improved communication between people. Others believe it has actually reduced face-to-face interaction. **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**

**Points for each view:**
- Improved: global connection, accessibility
- Reduced: less in-person interaction, superficial relationships

---

#### Prompt 2.4
> Some people think that young children should not be allowed to use smartphones or tablets. Others believe technology is essential for children's development. **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**

**Points for each view:**
- Against: health, addiction, social skills
- For: educational apps, digital literacy, future preparation

---

### Two-Part Questions

#### Prompt 2.5
> More and more people are working from home using technology. **Why is this happening? Do you think this is a positive or negative development?**

**Reasons:** technology advancement, COVID-19, cost savings
**Evaluation:** flexibility vs isolation, productivity vs work-life balance

---

## 3. Environment (Môi trường)

### Problem-Solution Essays

#### Prompt 3.1
> Environmental pollution is becoming an increasingly serious problem in many cities around the world. **What are the causes of this problem? What solutions can you suggest?**

**Causes:** industrialization, vehicles, waste management
**Solutions:** regulations, public transport, awareness

---

#### Prompt 3.2
> Global warming is one of the biggest threats facing the world today. **What are the causes? What can governments and individuals do to address this issue?**

**Causes:** fossil fuels, deforestation, agriculture
**Solutions:** renewable energy, international agreements, lifestyle changes

---

### Opinion Essays

#### Prompt 3.3
> Some people believe that individuals can do nothing to protect the environment and that it is the responsibility of governments and large companies. **Do you agree or disagree?**

**Key arguments:**
- Individuals matter: collective action, consumer choices
- vs. Systemic change: policy, corporate responsibility

---

### Discussion Essays

#### Prompt 3.4
> Some people think that economic growth is more important than protecting the environment. Others believe that the environment should be the priority. **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**

**Points for each view:**
- Economic growth: jobs, poverty reduction, development
- Environment: sustainability, long-term thinking, health

---

#### Prompt 3.5
> Some people think that nuclear energy is the solution to climate change. Others believe it is too dangerous. **What are the advantages and disadvantages of nuclear power?**

**Advantages:** clean energy, reliable, efficient
**Disadvantages:** safety risks, waste, cost

## How to Use This Prompt Bank

### Timed Practice
1. Chọn 1 prompt ngẫu nhiên
2. Phân tích đề (3 phút): Xác định dạng bài + yêu cầu
3. Lập dàn ý (5 phút)
4. Viết bài (27 phút)
5. Kiểm tra (5 phút)

### Dạng Bài Recognition
| Dấu hiệu | Dạng bài |
|----------|----------|
| "agree or disagree" | Opinion |
| "discuss both views" | Discussion |
| "causes...solutions" | Problem-Solution |
| "advantages and disadvantages" | Advantages-Disadvantages |
| 2 câu hỏi riêng biệt | Two-Part Question |

### Revision Strategy
1. **Week 1-2**: Làm 1 prompt mỗi ngày trong mỗi dạng
2. **Week 3-4**: Tăng lên 2 prompts/ngày, đa dạng chủ đề
3. **Week 5+**: Thi thử với điều kiện thực tế

### Self-Assessment Checklist
- [ ] Đã xác định đúng dạng bài?
- [ ] Thesis statement rõ ràng?
- [ ] Có ít nhất 2 main ideas developed?
- [ ] Mỗi ý có example/evidence?
- [ ] Conclusion tóm tắt + final thought?
- [ ] Đủ 250+ từ?
- [ ] Không lạc đề?
