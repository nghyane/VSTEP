# VSTEP Writing Task 1 - Prompt Bank (Ngân Hàng Đề)

> **Task 1**: Letter/Email | **Thời gian**: 20 phút | **Số từ**: 120+

---

## 1. Complaint Letters (Thư Phàn Nàn)

### Prompt 1.1 - Faulty Product
> You recently bought a new mobile phone, but it has some problems. Write a letter to the shop manager. In your letter:
> - describe the product and when you bought it
> - explain the problems you are having
> - say what you want the shop to do

**Key points to cover:**
- Product details (model, date, price)
- Specific problems (battery, screen, software)
- Request (refund, replacement, repair)

---

### Prompt 1.2 - Poor Service
> You stayed at a hotel last month and were very disappointed with the service. Write a letter to the hotel manager. In your letter:
> - say when you stayed and what room you had
> - explain why you were disappointed
> - suggest what the hotel should do to improve

**Key points to cover:**
- Booking details (dates, room type)
- Service issues (staff, cleanliness, facilities)
- Constructive suggestions

---

### Prompt 1.3 - Noisy Neighbors

> - explain how it affects you
> - suggest what the landlord could do

**Key points to cover:**
- Type of noise (music, parties, pets)
- Impact (sleep, work, stress)
- Possible solutions

---

## How to Use This Prompt Bank

### For Practice
1. Chọn 1 prompt ngẫu nhiên
2. Đặt timer 20 phút
3. Viết bài hoàn chỉnh (120+ từ)
4. Kiểm tra checklist bên dưới

### Checklist Cho Mỗi Bài
- [ ] Đã đáp ứng TẤT CẢ bullet points?
- [ ] Format đúng (Dear..., Yours...)?
- [ ] Giọng văn phù hợp (formal/semi-formal)?
- [ ] Đủ 120 từ?
- [ ] Chia đoạn rõ ràng?

### Level Progression
- **B1**: Làm các prompt cơ bản (1-2 trong mỗi loại)
- **B2**: Làm đa dạng các loại, tăng độ phức tạp
- **C1**: Làm tất cả, yêu cầu ngôn ngữ sophisticated
