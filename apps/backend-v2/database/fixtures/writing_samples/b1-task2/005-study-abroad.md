Nowadays many student want to study abroad. In my opinion, studying in other country has both good and bad sides. On the one hand, there are many advantages of studying abroad. Firstly, student can to learn a new language much faster. When they live in a foreign country, they have to speak English every day so they improve quickly. Secondly, studying abroad help young people become more independent. They have to take care of themselves like cooking, cleaning and managing money. These skill are very important for their future. Finally, they can know about other culture and make friends from around the world. On the other hand, there are some problems. Studying abroad is very expensive. People has to pay for tuition, accommodation and flight ticket. Many family cannot afford this. Also, some student feel lonely and homesick because they miss their family and friend. Another problem is that the food and weather might be different from their home country and make them sick. In conclusion, I think studying abroad has more advantages than disadvantages. However, student should prepare very well before they go.
