Nowadays many people do online shopping. It is very popular all around the world. There are some good things about online shopping. First, it is easy and we can buy things at home. We dont need to go to shop or travel long distance. This save us a lot of time. Second, online shopping have many products and we can see many things from different countries. We can compare the price and choose the best one. Third, we can shop at any time, even at midnight. But there are also bad things. Sometimes we buy something and it is not the same as the picture. We feel very disappointed. And we must wait for delivery. Sometimes it take many days or even one week. This is not good when we need something quickly. And we cannot try the clothes before we buy, so sometimes the size is wrong. Another problem is that bad people can steal our personal information from the internet. I think online shopping is good but we need to be careful. We should check the product before we buy and read reviews from other customers.
