Nowadays young people is spending too much time on their smartphone. This is a big problem that many family worry about. There are many bad things about using phone too much. First, it make us to feel very tired. When we look at the phone screen for many hour, our eyes become dry and hurt. Also our neck and back can hurt because we always look down. Second, it affect our study and work. When we use phone too much, we dont have no time to study or do our homework. Many student get bad mark because they play game or watch video instead of study. Third, using phone too much can damage our relationship. We talk to people online but we forget to talk to our family in real life. To solve this problem, I think young people should limit their time on phone. For example, they can set a timer and only use phone for two hours a day. Parents also should help their children to use phone less. In conclusion, using smartphone too much is not good for health and life. I believe everyone should control their phone use.
