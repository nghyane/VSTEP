Many student in university today has a part-time job. This is very common. I think it has good things and bad things. First, working part-time has some advantages. Student can earn money to pay for their study and living cost. They do not need to ask their parent for money all the time. Also, working help student get experience for their future career. It teach them how to work with other people and how to manage time. Some student even find a job that relate to what they study at school. However, there are also some disadvantages. When student spend many hour working, they have less time for study. They might get bad grade because they cannot finish their homework. Another problem is that student can feel very tired. If they go to class in the morning and work in the evening, they doesn't have enough time to rest and relax. This is not good for their health. In my opinion, I think working part-time is good if student can balance between work and study. University should help student to find suitable job that do not affect their study too much.
