Unemployment is a big problem for young people in many countries today. When young people cannot find job, they has many difficulties. They cannot earn money so they depend on their family. This make them feel very bad and stressed. They also lose their confidence because they try many time but no one hire them. Some young people stay at home for long time and they forget what they learned in school. I think government should help them to find job. Government should make many job for young people by building big projects like roads and bridges. Also government can give money to companies so they hire more workers. Young people also need to learn more skill to get job. If they have skill like computer or English they can have better job. I think this problem is very important for our society. If young people have job, they can have good life. Government and young people must work together to solve this problem.
