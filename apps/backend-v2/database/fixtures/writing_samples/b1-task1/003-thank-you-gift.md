Dear Trang,

I hope you are doing well. I want to say thank you for the lovely birthday gift you sent me. I was so surprise when I received it because I did not expect anything. The bag you gave me is very beautifuls and I use it every day now. My friend at school also said it look very nice on me. I was so happy when I open the package and saw the gift inside. It made my birthday very special. You are a really good friend and I am very grateful to have you. I want to see you soon because it has been long time since we last met. Why don't you come to visit me this summer? We can go to many interesting places together. I am sure we will have a lot of fun. Please let me know when you are free.

Your friend,
Nga
