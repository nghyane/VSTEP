Dear Hoa,

I hope you are well. I want to tell you about my trip last weekend. I went to Dalat with my family. Dalat is a beautiful city in the mountains with cool weather. The weather was very nice and fresh there. We went to the flower garden and take many photos of colorful flowers. The flowers was very beautiful with many kinds like roses and sunflowers. We also drink strawberry smoothie because Dalat is famous for strawberry. It was very delicious and sweet. In the afternoon we visited a beautiful waterfall and the view was amazing. In the evening we went to the night market and eat many delicious food like grilled rice paper and soy milk. We also buy some souvenirs for our friends. I felt very happy and relaxing during the trip. I like Dalat very much and I want to come back someday. Do you want to go with me next time? It will be fun.

Write back soon.
Your friend,
Mai
